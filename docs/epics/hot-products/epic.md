# Epic: Hot Products

## Business Goal

Allow admins to promote select listings to the "Hot" category for increased
visibility.

## Problem Statement

High-potential or urgent listings need to stand out from the hundreds of
active listings. Without a promotion feature, all listings have equal
visibility.

## Business Value

- Drives attention to priority listings
- Helps move high-commission or time-sensitive deals faster
- Gives admin a merchandising tool

## Actors

- Admin

## Scope

### In Scope

- Mark/unmark listing as Hot
- Hot filter tab on homepage
- "🔥 HOT" badge on listing cards
- Hot list management page (admin)
- Hot listings appear first in grid

### Out of Scope

- Time-limited hot promotions
- Paid promotion slots

## Features

- Toggle hot status on listings
- Admin management page for hot products
- Badge on homepage cards

## Dependencies

- Listing Management

## Business Rules

- BR-003 Hot listings appear at the top of the listing grid.
